## Get tracking running in three steps

<Steps>

<Step title="Create a Tracking Container">

A **container** is the top-level entity in Metrito that isolates all your tracking data, sources, and destinations. Each container has a unique identifier in the format `MTC-XXXXXXX` (similar to GTM container IDs).

1. Log in to the [Metrito Dashboard](https://app.metrito.com)
2. Navigate to **Settings** > **Tracking Container**
3. Create a new container for your domain
4. Copy the **Container ID** (e.g., `MTC-5X35GWQ`)

<Tip>Each container is fully isolated — events, leads, and attribution data never mix between containers.</Tip>

</Step>

<Step title="Install the Tracking Pixel">

Add the Metrito tracking script to your website. Paste this snippet before the closing `</head>` tag:

```html
<script>
(function(w,d,u,h,k){var o="mtrt";w[o]=w[o]||function(){
(w[o].queue=w[o].queue||[]).push(arguments)};w[o].ver=1;w[o].queue=[];
var s=d.createElement("script");s.async=1;
s.src=h+"/${o}tag.js";d.head.appendChild(s)
})(window,document,new URL(location),"https://sst.yourdomain.com","MTC-XXXXXXX");
</script>
```

Replace:
- `sst.yourdomain.com` with your server-side tagging domain (or use `api.metrito.com` as fallback)
- `MTC-XXXXXXX` with your container ID

<Info>The pixel automatically captures page views, UTM parameters, referrer, cookies (`_fbp`, `_ga`), and device information.</Info>

See the full [Web Pixel Setup](/tracking/web-setup) guide for details.

</Step>

<Step title="Configure UTM Parameters">

Add these URL parameters to your Meta Ads campaigns so Metrito can attribute conversions to the right campaign, ad set, and ad:

```
utm_source=facebook&utm_campaign={{campaign.name}}|{{campaign.id}}&utm_medium={{adset.name}}|{{adset.id}}&utm_content={{ad.name}}|{{ad.id}}&utm_term={{placement}}
```

In **Meta Ads Manager**: Campaign > Edit > Tracking > URL Parameters.

Metrito auto-parses the `name|id` format to extract both human-readable names and technical IDs for reporting.

See the full [UTM Configuration](/tracking/utm-configuration) guide for Meta, Google, and TikTok setup.

</Step>

</Steps>

## What happens next

Once installed, Metrito begins tracking immediately:

- **Page views** are captured with full UTM attribution
- **Leads** are identified when email or phone is submitted through forms
- **Sessions** are created and linked to journeys for multi-touch attribution
- **Cross-channel identity** links anonymous web visits to WhatsApp conversations and purchases
